About Zenix

A cleaner way to shop curated essentials.

Zenix Store is built as a production-quality static ecommerce demo with polished browsing, account, cart, checkout, and order flows.

What Zenix focuses on

Zenix brings together a focused catalog across technology, fashion, beauty, furniture, and home. The app keeps the experience direct: find products quickly, save what you like, checkout with validated details, and keep a clean invoice trail.

Quality catalog

Every product has structured data, stock, ratings, descriptions, and features.

Account first

Profiles and order history stay tied to the signed-in browser account.

Accessible UI

Forms, buttons, navigation, and messages are keyboard and screen-reader friendly.

Static deploy

The app can be hosted by any static web host without a build step.
